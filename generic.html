Overview

I analyzed data from Google containing information on nearly 1.2 million apps to determine the most popular apps in the market in June 2021. The data included aspects such as the name, category, average rating, and total downloads of each app.

The first step in this project was to clean and preprocess the data using Power Query. This involved removing any irrelevant or duplicate entries, transforming the data into a usable format, and standardizing the data to ensure accuracy.

Next, I used Power BI to visualize and explore the data. I created various visualizations such as bar charts, line charts, and cards to get an overview of the data and identify any trends or patterns. I also used the DAX language in Power BI to create measures, which allowed me to import icons and images to make my report look more presentable.

To determine the most popular apps, I weighted the average rating of each app by its total downloads, as this gave a better indication of both the popularity and quality of each app. I then ranked the apps based on this weighted average and identified the top apps as the most popular. You can also explore top apps by category using the category slicer.

The results of this project provided valuable insights into the app market, which can be used by developers, marketers, and investors to make informed decisions. By using Power BI, I was able to effectively visualize and analyze the data, resulting in a comprehensive and easy-to-understand report. Overall, this project demonstrated the power of data analysis and the benefits of using Power BI for data exploration and reporting.
